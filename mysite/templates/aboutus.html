About Us

Know about Rokto Lagbe. What, how, and why.

Rokto Lagbe is an automated blood service that connects blood searchers with voluntary blood donors in a moment through SMS and website. Rokto is a free service for all. Rokto started its journey in 2018.

Why Rokto Lagbe?

Though Bangladesh has more than 160 million people, the number of safe blood banks is very few. Without divisional towns, there is hardly any blood bank. But a huge amount of blood is needed for treatment purposes...

Rokto comes into the scenario to reduce or minimize the communication gap and connects people in a moment using the amazing power of SMS and email...

Vision

Ensuring no more death just for the need of blood

Mission

Connecting blood searchers with voluntary blood donors in a moment with the use of technology.

Objectives

  • Encouraging voluntary blood donation
  • Creating awareness about safe blood transfer
  • Enabling people to place blood request via SMS, website, or Facebook
  • Connecting voluntary blood donors via SMS or email
